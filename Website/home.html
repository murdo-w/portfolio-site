HTML5 Markup

HTML5 is the backbone to all websites. It describes what is actually on the webpage. HTML5 works in synergy with CSS3 and JavaScript.

CSS3 Styling

CSS3 describes what everything looks like and where they are positioned on the webpage. If there was no styling, it would look like a text document, which wouldn't be very nice.
